About Us

Manufacturer & Bulk Supplier of Dehydrated Vegetable Powders

Who We Are

Swarupa Agro Food Processing is an Indian manufacturer and bulk supplier of dehydrated vegetable powders and flakes. We specialize in processing high-quality agricultural produce into export-ready ingredients for food manufacturers, nutraceutical companies, and international buyers.

Our product range includes spinach powder, moringa leaf powder, onion powder, garlic powder, ginger powder, and customized dehydrated vegetables based on buyer specifications.

We focus on hygiene, consistency, and long-term partnerships with our clients. Each batch is processed under controlled conditions to ensure food safety, uniform quality, and compliance with market requirements.
